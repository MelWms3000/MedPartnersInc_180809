4 STEPS TO FILING A CLAIM

1

Report Your Injury

Report your injury to your supervisor immediately. Your employer has only 4 hour​s ​to​ ​provide your ​​forms​ CA-1, CA-16 & CA-17 listed in step #2.

2

Request Completed Forms

Request Completed forms CA-1, CA-16, and CA-17.​ Please note: You have only 7​ ​days​ from the date of your injury ​to request form CA-16.

3

Notify Your Union Steward

Report your injury to your Union​ ​Steward​. Tell your union steward about your claim as soon as possible.

4

Contact MedPartners, Inc.

Schedule an appointment today!
