What is Continuation of Pay? Continuation of Pay is 100% of salary entitled and paid to
an injured Federal worker when wounded on the job.
Who is eligible for Continuation of Pay?Any injured Federal Worker whose injured and files a
form CA-1.

How long can I receive Continuation of Pay?		One can receive Continuation of Pay for up to 45 days.
If I don't use ALL of my Continuation of Pay, can I use the remaining balance if I am reinjured?Yes you can.

What is a form CA-16? A form CA-16 gives authorization for IMMEDIATE TREATMENT
without pre-authorization requirements.
How does an injured federal employee obtain a form CA-16?An injured Federal Employee has to only REQUEST THE FORM
from upper management in the event that an injury is incurred.
How long, after an injury, do I have to request a form CA-16? An injured Federal Employee has 7 days from the date of injury to formally request a form CA-16 from their Supervisor/upper management.
Is there any regulation(s) the supervisor/upper management must adhere to regarding an injured Federal Employee's request for form CA-16?YES. 20 CFR 10.300- states that the employer shall issue an employee a form CA-16 within 4HOURS of the claimed injury.
