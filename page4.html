What We Do

Case Management

We will coordinate services on behalf of your individual needs. Customer care is paramount at MedPartners, Inc.

Range of Motion (ROM)

Range of Motion, ROM, is the range through which a joint can be moved. Before your injury, you usually moved your joints many times during the day. After an injury, you may move less.
Our certified staff will help you determine your limits and work towards achieving your goals.

Pain Management

Your injury can be complex. We are prepared to assist you with pain management. We provide medications and therapies to get you back to work.

Diagnostic Testing

A diagnostic test is a procedure performed to confirm or determine the presence of your injury:
MRIs
X-Rays
CT Scans
