Are you an Injured federal employee?

MedPartners, Inc will assist you with Department of Labor Office of Workers' Compensation filing and management. Our trained staff are here to assist you.

You should know all of the rights given by the Federal Employees Compensation Act (FECA). 

MedPartners, Inc provides materials to help you learn your rights as a federal employee.

Let MedPartners, Inc help you navigate the OWCP claims process. 

We will make sure that you never feel like you are alone, while going through the process. Let MedPartners, Inc. take care of your medical and case management needs.

There is never a cost for our services to you. 

This is MedPartners, Inc. guarantee to you. MedPartners, Inc. will handle all OWCP paperwork, while you focus on healing and returning to a normal lifestyle.
