Department of Labor (DOL) Forms

CA-1

Federal Employee's Notice of Traumatic Injury and Claim for Continuation of Pay/Compensation

When you sustain a traumatic injury that is work related which includes an aggravation of a pre-existing condition, complete the employee’s portion of form CA-1.

CA-16

Authorization for Examination And/Or Treatment

A CA-16 should be requested from your supervisor when an employee sustains a traumatic injury on the job. Include this clause: Please Note: The completed and signed CA-16 form is given to the requesting employee within 4 hours of the reported injury.

CA-17

Daily Status Report

This form CA-17 is used to request work restrictions after an injury has been sustained.
Please note: Side A must be completed by your supervisor before the treating physician completes Side B.
